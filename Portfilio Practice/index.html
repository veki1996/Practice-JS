Frontend Web Developer Front-end deals with client aspects, ie presenting content to the user in the window of his web browser. So I consider the front-end a design issue. Frontend developers are in charge of creating, programming, and customizing the complete visual identity of a site or web application. In other words, their job is to provide the functionality and interactivity of all the elements you see on a single website. On the other hand, the role of frontend developers is to design a user interface that will be adapted to different devices, but also users.
